Presented in Session 53: Great Lakes Water Level Fluctuations and Water Management. Co-authors: Vincent Cheng, Dong-Kyun Kim, Alex Neumann, Agnes Richards, and George Arhonditsis (University of Toronto Scarborough and Environment Canada). This talk investigated the connections between inter-annual and multi-decadal water level fluctuations in the Laurentian Great Lakes and large-scale climate teleconnections and circulation patterns, exploring how global climate variability propagates into the regional hydrological cycle of the Great Lakes basin.
