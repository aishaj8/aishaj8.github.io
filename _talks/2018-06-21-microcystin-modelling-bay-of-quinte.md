Presented in Session 06: Ecosystem Health and Recovery of the Bay of Quinte, Lake Ontario. Co-authors: Yuko Shimoda, Noreen Kelly, Susan Watson, Arthur Zastepa, Dong-Kyun Kim, and George Arhonditsis. This presentation introduced a probabilistic modelling framework to assess the likelihood of microcystin concentration exceedances relative to Health Canada drinking water and recreational safety guidelines in the Bay of Quinte. The work formed the foundation for the later published Bayesian risk assessment framework appearing in *Water Research* (2019).
